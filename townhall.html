關於我們 About

舊金山中文沉浸學校之友是一個由家長組成的倡議團體，致力推動舊金山聯合學區於2027–2028學年開設K–8普通話沉浸式學校。

Friends of the SF Mandarin School is a parent-led group working with SFUSD to launch a dedicated Mandarin Immersion K–8 school for the 2027–2028 school year.
