How You Can Help

Submitted the interest form and ready for more? Here are concrete ways to help right now.

  • Tell your preschool or daycare Share the site with families at your child's school, especially Mandarin immersion programs

Make Connections

  • Introduce us to leaders Connect us with political leaders, community leaders, or democratic clubs

Share Your Expertise

  • Legal, finance, or nonprofit Help us navigate nonprofit setup, fiscal sponsorship, or governance
  • Media, PR, or social media Lend your skills to help us tell this story more broadly
  • Education or school admin Share your knowledge of how schools launch and what makes them succeed
