# Mandarin Website — Project Context

## What This Is

A one-page website for **Friends of the SF Mandarin School**, a parent-led community group advocating for and organizing around SFUSD's commitment to launch a Mandarin Immersion K-8 school for the 2027-2028 school year.

**Domain:** sfmandarinschool.org

## Design Direction

- Style: polished NY agency — clean, white, bold typography, refined
- Color palette: white base with a bold accent (deep red or jade green, TBD)
- No logo yet — use school name as wordmark for now
